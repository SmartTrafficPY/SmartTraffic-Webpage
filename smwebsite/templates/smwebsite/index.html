{% trans 'Project Goals' %}

{% trans 'Primary Goal' %}

{% trans 'Research, design, and implementation of technologies that exploit the advantages of Collective Adaptative Systems (CAS) and Mobile Crowdsensing (MCS) to improve the urban mobility of Asunción, the capital city of Paraguay.' %}

{% trans 'Mobile crowdsensing (MCS), or simply crowdsensing, is a sensing paradigm that uses the power of various mobile devices, such as smartphones, tablets, wearable devices, smart sensors, etc., to monitor large-scale phenomena.' %}

{% trans 'Collective Adaptive Systems (CAS) are systems that consist of multiple components or agents that interact collaboratively on common goals, and compete to achieve individual goals. The key notion is that although these agents identify with each other as a group or collective, they lack a complete view of the system and all decisions they take must rely only on partial, localized information.' %}

{% trans 'Specific Goals' %}

{% trans 'Understand the start of the art on CAS- and MCS-based technologies proposed to address urban mobility problems.' %}

{% trans 'Model, design, and implement solutions that leverage on CAS and MCS technologies to improve the urban mobility of Asuncion, Paraguay.' %}

{% trans 'Validate the solutions as efficient platforms to solve problems in urban mobility.' %}

{% trans 'Expected Results' %}

{% trans 'Report' %}

{% trans 'The report "SmartCity Asunción" where the state of the art related to the different models and technologies related to SmartCities is compiled and its possible application and utility for the city of Asunción is studied.' %}

{% trans 'Design' %}

{% trans 'A proposal of architectural design of the SmartCity platform adapted to the demands of Asunción, product of research, adaptation and contribution to the state of the art of the models and related technologies, in line with the areas of interest identified in the previous report.' %}

{% trans 'Prototype' %}

{% trans 'The implementation of the prototype platform "SmartCity of Asunción" that offers services of registration and processing of the collected data, and the implementation of SmartTraffic and other demonstrative applications to collect input data from each of the mobile sensor units and present the joint result of monitoring when it is required by the user.' %}

{% trans 'Test' %}

{% trans 'A test report, validation and evaluation of the results of the prototype platform and the SmartTraffic demonstration application, using various types of empirical studies for the usability analysis and other attributes that determine the quality of a product.' %}

{% trans 'Dissemination of results' %}

{% trans 'Dissemination of results and software tools resulting in web and other social media. Consolidation of research: publication in congresses and international journals. Other general and / or specialized disseminating activities.' %}
