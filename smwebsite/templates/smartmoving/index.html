Smartmoving

{% trans 'Improving mobility for pedestrians, providing information on the condition of the trails and recommending roads with fewer obstacles to a destination' %}

{% trans 'SmartMoving Aplication' %}

{% trans 'According to data obtained from the municipality of Asunción, in 2018 there were a total of 1836 complaints about the sidewalks, of which 749 were about obstruction and occupation of sidewalks and 686 where about construction and repair of sidewalks.' %}

{% trans 'On behalf of the SmartTraffic project, a citizenship survey was conducted to find the problems a citizen faces in Asunción. In the survey the 73% responded that the state of the sidewalks is a serious or very serious problem' %}

{% trans 'So, taking the results of the survey and the large number of complaints made through the municipality, it was seen as an opportunity to make a mobile application system that offers information about the state of sidewalks and road recommendations.' %}

{% trans 'Primary Goal' %}

  • {% trans 'Develop an application to provide the user with information about the state of the sidewalks near their current location and recommend paths with fewer obstacles to a destination. It would benefit people who have reduced mobility such as people in a wheelchair, walker, cane and with a baby carriage' %}

{% trans 'Specific Goals' %}

  • {% trans 'Study the techniques and strategies that apply to the smartmoving solution' %}
  • {% trans 'Apply the user-centered design methodology to perform the following steps iteratively:' %}
    • {% trans 'Design the solution and its architecture' %}
    • {% trans 'Develop a prototype' %}
    • {% trans 'Evaluate the prototype, qualitatively with users, in order to know their impression of its usefulness' %}
