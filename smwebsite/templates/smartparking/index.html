SmartParking

{% trans 'Improving congestion by helping the user find a place to park' %}

{% trans 'SmartParking application' %}

{% trans 'Asunción enters about one and a half million people daily for different reasons. The impossibility of the road network to meet the demand that this massive mobilization entails generates several problems related to the low average speed of travel (approximately 15 km/h), a high stress and loss of time in the population that moves, increasing the fuel consumption and the consequent increase in the emission of toxic agents.' %}

{% trans 'Primary Goal' %}

  • {% trans 'Design a mobile application that facilitates the search for well-defined parking places and enabled by the relevant authorities' %}

{% trans 'Benefits' %}

  • {% trans 'It will benefit people in terms of saving fuel and time' %}

  • {% trans 'It is considered that 30% of the traffic congestion in an area is generated by people in search of a place to park' %}

  • {% trans 'Experts indicate parking collapse in Asunción, if this problem is not addressed as soon as possible' %}

{% trans 'Specific Goals' %}

  • {% trans 'Study of the techniques and strategies to be used in the implementation of systems focused on solving parking place problems' %}

  • {% trans 'Design of a solution focused on the problem of parking spot search' %}

  • {% trans 'Implementation of the previously designed solution' %}
