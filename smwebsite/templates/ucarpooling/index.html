UCArpooling

{% trans 'Improving congestion by reducing the number of vehicles on the streets, at the same time providing security' %}

{% trans 'UCArpooling Application' %}

{% trans 'Asunción suffers serious mobility problems at the time of commuting because it does not offer support for the number of citizens, where 1,320,000 people enter per day. Taking into account the number of vehicles on the streets and the average number of people per private vehicle is 1.2 to 1.5, it concludes that the use of transport is not optimized resulting in long lines of vehicles, loss of money and pollution to the environment.' %}

{% trans 'On behalf of the SmartTraffic project, a citizenship survey was conducted to find the problems a citizen faces in Asunción regarding their daily mobility. The survey resulted in the majority, with 73% of respondents, using their own car as the main means of transportation.' %}

{% trans 'In relation to the other transport alternatives in Asunción, public transport has a poor service, where most of the bus fleet has mechanical problems during its routines, or the traffic schedule ends early. It should also be noted that the only public transport option is the bus. About the use of bicycles, this has its difficulties since there is only in selected areas the infrastructure necessary for its safe circulation, such as the bike path.' %}

{% trans 'So, taking the low average number of people per vehicle, plus most people move in their own car and the drawbacks already mentioned in relation to the possible alternatives, it was seen as an opportunity to make a mobile application system that offers carpooling pairing to people from the same environment.' %}

{% trans 'Primary Goal' %}

  • {% trans 'Design a non-profit carpooling system with a mobile application aimed at closed communities, decreasing the number of vehicles while commuting.' %}

{% trans 'Benefits' %}

  • {% trans 'It would benefit the city by reducing the number of vehicles on the road and everything that this causes, like less waiting time at traffic lights, especially during peak hours.' %}
  • {% trans 'It would benefit the institution that adopts it, by improving the camaraderie among its members and would help with difficulties of parking spaces' %}
  • {% trans 'It would benefit people by saving fuel money and generating new friendships and connections' %}

{% trans 'Specific Goals' %}

  • {% trans 'Study the techniques and strategies that apply to the situation of a carpooling system for users of the same institution' %}
  • {% trans 'Design the carpooling system taking into account what has been studied' %}
  • {% trans 'Implement the matcher module between people for the system' %}
  • {% trans 'Analyze the impact of such system in a closed community. In particular at the Catholic University "Nuestra señora de la Asunción"' %}
